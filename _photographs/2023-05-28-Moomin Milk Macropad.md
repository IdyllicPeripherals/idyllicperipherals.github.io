### Commissioned Acrylic Case

Build Specifications:
* DSA Keycaps
* Acrylic Case
* Milk 2% PCB
* Sea Micro
* SP Star Switches
* 0305 Mill Max Sockets

This macropad uses VIA and is easy to program. I also installed RGB underglow to the PCB.
